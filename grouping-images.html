Group the given figures into three classes using each figure only once.
Grouping Strategy: Look for patterns like:
  • Number of lines or components (e.g., all figures made of 3 lines).
  • Presence of internal marks (e.g., all figures containing a small dot).
  • Shapes within shapes (e.g., a triangle inside a circle).
  • Symmetry or Identical Parts.
